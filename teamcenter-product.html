Overview

The Teamcenter product lifecycle management (PLM) solution suite from Siemens PLM Software helps integrating data, processes, business systems and, ultimately, people in an extended enterprise. By providing the right information at the right time and optimizing relationships along the product lifecycle, Teamcenter enables companies to make better product decisions from planning and development through manufacturing and support.

Offerings

Design
What Is Solid Edge

Teamcenter enables you to manage and share cross-domain design information, including mechanical, electronics, software and simulation in a single environment and automates your engineering change, validation and approval processes to speed cycle time and improve product quality.

Know More

Simulation
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge Simulation software is an easy to use, built-in finite element analysis tool that enables design engineers to digitally validate part and assembly designs within the Solid Edge environment. Simulation capabilities of Solid Edge include simulation of individual parts, assembly analysis, definition and analysis of complete systems and computational fluid dynamics (CFD).

Manufacturing
What Is Solid Edge

Teamcenter enables a truly concurrent engineering environment that allows you to leverage product designs while simultaneously optimizing and synchronizing manufacturing deliverables. By establishing a single source of product and process knowledge, Teamcenter facilitates re-using of best practices and manage resources for continuous improvement.

Technical Publications
What Is Solid Edge

Teamcenter provides documentation tools to help your teams create specifications, analysis reports, 2D/3D illustrations, spreadsheets, test results, and technical publications. With easy-to-use document management and technical authoring and publishing tools, Teamcenter keeps your product design and documentation aligned with product changes to reduce the time and cost of development.

Data Management
What Is Solid Edge

Teamcenter enables you to manage, control and share product, process, resource and plant information across your entire design and supply chain. By establishing a single source of product and process knowledge, Teamcenter allows manufacturing companies to re-use best practices and manage resources for continuous improvement.

Apps
What Is Solid Edge

Teamcenter Mobility application (available on Apple App Store) provides your mobile workforce with a secure and easy access to PLM environment from their mobile device anywhere, anytime. Mobilized teams, with access to real-time data can attain better responsiveness by decreasing the time needed to respond to rising issues.

Industry Solution
What Is Solid Edge
