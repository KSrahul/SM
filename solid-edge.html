Overview

Solid Edge is a portfolio of affordable, easy-to-use software tools that address all aspects of the product development process. Solid Edge is the most comprehensive and scalable digital product development system with powerful capabilities ranging from 2D Drafting to an advanced 3D system – complete with assembly design, automated drawing production, simulation and assembly applications.

Offerings

Design
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge uses synchronous technology to help you accelerate product design, make revisions faster, and improve design data re-use. By integrating the mechanical, electrical and electronic aspects of product design you can effectively get products to market faster, better service customer needs and reduce engineering design costs.

Know More

Simulation
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge Simulation software is an easy to use, built-in finite element analysis tool that enables design engineers to digitally validate part and assembly designs within the Solid Edge environment. Simulation capabilities of Solid Edge include simulation of individual parts, assembly analysis, definition and analysis of complete systems and computational fluid dynamics (CFD).

Manufacturing
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge manufacturing solutions help manufacturers to define and execute a wide range of traditional and new manufacturing processes including CNC machining, nesting, cutting, bending, molding, welding, assembling, and additive manufacturing. Solid Edge allows you to maximize the value of your investments in the latest, most efficient and most capable machine tools.

Technical Publications
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge offers a full suite of tools that lets designers author, edit, distribute and explore design alternatives and technical documents quickly and easily.  With design to publication integration and ability to perform rapid and flexible edits, Solid Edge reduces the need for specialist technical authors or external services and enables producing top quality documents in-house.

Data Management
What Is Solid Edge

Scalable data management solutions from Solid Edge enables you to effectively work with the fast-growing volumes of data created in product designing and manufacturing process. Pervasive integration with Microsoft SharePoint and Teamcenter gives you the ability to manage data across the entire product development cycle helping you to maximize productivity and streamline global operations.

Apps
What Is Solid Edge

The Solid Edge App Marketplace mobile app helps customers easily search for, compare and purchase partner developed apps for Solid Edge software. Solid Edge apps available on a variety of platforms extend the capabilities of Solid Edge to provide an efficient collaborative process among your team, your customers and suppliers with access to information anywhere and at any time on the device of their choice.

Industry Solution
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge provides users with world-class part modeling, assembly design and engineering drawing solutions to meet a wide range of business needs across industries. Solid Edge combines the speed and simplicity of direct modeling with the flexibility and control of parametric design get products to deliver the fastest, most flexible design experience possible while easing the challenges inherit in performing product development.

Academic Program
What Is Solid Edge

Solid Edge Student Edition is a free download available to students or instructors to help them learn engineering principles by practicing software’s same core functionality used by leading engineers. Siemens also provides students with free access to topic-based or project-based tutorials, online self-paced courses and interactive learning resources and also offers Solid Edge Certification that gives them competitive edge when applying to jobs.
